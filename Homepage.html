    Why Double Treble?

    So... why buy our products?

    Do you play a musical Instrument? And don't you hate it when you are playing a longer peice and then in the middle of an intense section you have to stop playing to turn the page, causing the sound quality to diminish? Well then look no further becasue our music stand clip-ons have got you covered!


    Our simple design complements the stand perfectly, meaning that you can just add it on to your existing music stand. Operated with a convinent foot pedal, It will turn the pages quickly and automatically without you having to stop playing. And for all you players who cannot press the pedal, we are looking into a recognition system that will recognise what song you are playing (regardless of your instrument!) and know when to turn for you. However, this system is still in planning.
